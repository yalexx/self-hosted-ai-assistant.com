When you type a message into ChatGPT, that text leaves your device, travels across the internet, lands on a server you don't control, gets processed by models you didn't train, and may contribute to future training runs. For casual questions this is acceptable. For business documents, personal health queries, confidential client data, or financial information — it's a serious risk. A self-hosted AI assistant processes everything locally. The data never leaves your device, period.
